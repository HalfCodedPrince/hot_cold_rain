# Ord Iron Marshes

## Basemap
- **Substrate:** peat/fen beds with iron-rich groundwater; mangrove tangles on brackish rims.
- **Water:** narrow, shifting channels.

## Hazards
Acid rot; fever belts; Green-Year surges contaminate channels.
